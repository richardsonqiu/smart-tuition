Requesting a tutor has never been easier at Smart tuition.

Simply visit our website and go to our Request a Tutor page, and fill up our form to request a tutor, it only takes less than 2 minutes finish.

You can also give us a quick call or email through our contact information below:
Email: request-tutors@smarttuition.com
Phone: +65 8123 4567

How do I request for a tutor?
After finishing our form to request a tutor, our staff will shortly find the best tutor available according to your preferences.

Upon matching, we will soon inform you about your newly assinged tutor via SMS and Email. If you have any questions regarding the assignment, feel free to drop us a call or email to clear up all your doubts.

How tutors are assigned?
We are here to find you the best tutor available for you.

If you feel unsatisfied with your current tutor, we are more than happy to attend to your request to get a different tutor. Please fill up our form on our Contact Us page to request for the change and we will find you a new tutor as quickly as possible.

Can I request for a different tutor?
Our tutors are selected from rigorous application process, where we ensure that they are well-qualified and passionate in teaching our students. They also go through regular in-house training to keep them up-to-date with the syllabus and continuous evaluation to ensure that they maintain quality teaching.

What can I expect from your tutors?
At Smart Tuition, we offer 3 different packages: Standard, Learner, and Scholar.

We specially crafted these packages to target all students with different needs alike, offering the best option for all of our students.

To learn more about each of the package, please visit our `Tuition Package` page.

How does your tuition package works?
If you are first time students applying a tutor at Smart Tuition, you are automatically elligible to 2 free lessons.

After requesting a tutor and being matched to a tutor, you can begin your 2 free lessons automatically.

We hope that you have a good experience using our tuition services, happy learning!

How does the free trial works?
Yes, you are free to cancel your tuition package anytime. There are no obligations for you to finish the whole period. Please drop us a call or email so we can talk you through our cancellation and refund process.

Can I cancel my tuition package anytime?
After finishing your free trial, we will send you a follow up email to ask whether you want to continue with your lessons. From here on, we will guide you thorugh the payment process, the payment is completely done through our secure online portal.
